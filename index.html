About Us

A Riverside Paradise

Nestled beside the pristine waters flowing from the majestic Dhuladhar mountain range, our cafe offers a unique dining experience that combines breathtaking natural beauty with exceptional cuisine. Since 2015, we've been serving locals and travelers with authentic flavors and warm hospitality.

Our philosophy is simple: fresh ingredients, traditional recipes with a modern twist, and an ambiance that lets you reconnect with nature. Whether you're seeking a peaceful morning coffee or a romantic dinner by the river, we create memorable moments.
