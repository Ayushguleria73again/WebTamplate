Our Story

A Riverside Retreat

Nestled along the serene banks of the Dhuladhar River, our cafe offers a tranquil escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. Founded in 2015, we've been serving carefully crafted dishes using locally-sourced ingredients.

Our philosophy is simple: great food, warm hospitality, and a connection to nature. The sound of the river and our signature dishes create an unforgettable experience for all our guests.
