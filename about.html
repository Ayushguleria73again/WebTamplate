Our Story

Discover the journey of Dhuladhar River Cafe

A Riverside Retreat

Nestled along the serene banks of the Dhuladhar River, our cafe offers a tranquil escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. Founded in 2015, we've been serving carefully crafted dishes using locally-sourced ingredients.

Our philosophy is simple: great food, warm hospitality, and a connection to nature. The sound of the river and our signature dishes create an unforgettable experience for all our guests.

Our Mission

To provide our guests with exceptional dining experiences that celebrate local flavors, sustainable practices, and the natural beauty of our riverside location. We believe in creating moments that nourish both body and soul.

Our Vision

To become the premier destination for travelers and locals seeking authentic cuisine, peaceful ambiance, and a genuine connection to the Himalayan landscape. We aim to set the standard for sustainable, community-focused dining.

Our Journey

2015 - The Beginning

Dhuladhar River Cafe opened its doors with a vision to create a unique riverside dining experience. Started by passionate food lovers who fell in love with this beautiful location.

2017 - Expansion

Added outdoor seating area to accommodate growing number of guests and to provide better river views. Introduced our signature Dhuladhar Thali.

2019 - Award Recognition

Received "Best Riverside Cafe" award from Himachal Tourism Board. Featured in multiple travel magazines and food blogs.

2021 - Sustainability Initiative

Launched our zero-waste kitchen program and partnered with local farmers for 100% organic produce. Installed solar panels for clean energy.

2023 - Present

Continuing to serve the community with love, expanding our menu with seasonal specials, and maintaining our commitment to sustainability and excellence.

Our Values

Sustainability
