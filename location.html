How to Reach Us

By Car

Take the main highway towards Dharamshala. Exit at Dhuladhar Bridge and follow signs for Riverside Road. Free parking available.

~ 30 min from city center

By Bus

Take bus route 12 or 15 from the main bus terminal. Get off at Dhuladhar Bridge stop and walk 5 minutes along the river path.

~ 45 min from bus terminal

By Taxi

Easily accessible by local taxis and ride-sharing apps. Simply enter "Dhuladhar River Cafe" as your destination.

Available 24/7
